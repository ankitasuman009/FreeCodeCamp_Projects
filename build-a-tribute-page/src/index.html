Chanu Saikhom Mirabai

Summer Olympic Games Tokyo 2020
Saikhom Mirabai Chanu won silver medal at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Women's 49 kg weightlifting.

Mirabai Chanu has won the World Championships and multiple medals at the Commonwealth Games.

Her achievements -

  • 2014 Scotland Glasgow, Scotland 48kg rank - 2nd
  • 2016 Brazil Rio de Janeiro, Brazil 48kg rank - 6th
  • 2017 United States Anaheim, United States 48 kg rank - 1st (gold medalist)
  • 2018 Australia Gold Coast, Australia 48kg rank 1st (gold medalist)
  • 2019 Thailand Pattaya, Thailand 49kg rank - 4th
  • 2019 China Ningbo, China 49kg rank - 4th
  • 2020 Uzbekistan Tashkent, Uzbekistan 49kg rank - 3rd
  • 2020 Japan Tokyo, Japan 49kg rank - 2nd
  • 2020 India Kolkata, India 49 kg rank - 1st (gold medalist)
